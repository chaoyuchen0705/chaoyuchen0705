Mixed-Use Redevelopment Proposal

The project aims to redevelop a strategic 85,724-square-foot site by demolishing and clearing the existing facilities to create a mixed-use development with multiple functions. Located in a newly established TIF (tax increment financing) area, the project includes plans for residences, apartments, commercial space, and a public parking lot. The development has a total floor area of 394,680 square feet and is planned to provide 215 apartments, accommodating about 404 residents, as well as 746 new jobs. The parking lot will provide 160 parking spaces to meet regional transportation needs.

The project is financed through TIF and is expected to contribute $19.046 million in tax increment by 2041. The total investment includes $2.678 million for public infrastructure improvements and $2 million for public parking facilities. In addition, the proposal not only increases the local housing supply and relieves the housing pressure in the community, but also optimizes transportation accessibility and enhances the vitality of the regional economy. The project makes full use of the financial incentives of the TIF area to ensure its economic feasibility and the overall interests of the community.
