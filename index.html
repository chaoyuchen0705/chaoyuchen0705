About Me

Hi, I’m Chaoyu Chen. I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a bachelor's degree in urban planning with a concentration in policy and planning.

During my studies, I gained in-depth knowledge of economics, politics, law, data modeling and analysis, and other fields, and combined with practical experience, I gained a deeper understanding of the operating principles of cities and societies. These learning experiences have equipped me with the ability to analyze problems and develop solutions from multiple dimensions. In my future career development, I hope to continue to learn new knowledge, improve my practical work ability, and create greater value for society through innovative planning and practice.

What I'm Doing

Urban Planning & Policy Design

I focus on urban planning and policy design, combining modern planning methods to carry out the overall design of land use, urban development and infrastructure construction to ensure that planning schemes can effectively promote sustainable urban development.

Urban Economic Analysis

I deeply study urban economics, and use economic theories and data analysis methods to analyze urban resource allocation, land use, public policy and financial management, so as to support scientific decision-making and policy optimization.

Data Modeling & Urban Forecasting

I apply data modeling and analysis techniques to forecast urban development trends, including population flow, transportation demand and environmental changes, and provide data-based decision support to promote smarter urban management and development.

Urban Law & Policy Compliance

I study laws and regulations related to urban development, ensure that all planning and policies comply with current legal requirements, and commit to providing compliance advice to governments and enterprises to ensure the legality and effectiveness of policy implementation.
